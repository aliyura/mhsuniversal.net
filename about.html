MHS Universal Investment Ltd

MHS Universal Investment Ltd is an Incooperative limited company founded by Mis. Smaeera Abubakar Abdullahi, a Female Entrepreneur with versatile experience in IT, Human Resources, Management Consultant, Women and Youth Social Development Specialist is a graduate of B.Ed. in Education Administration & planning from Winneba University of Education in Ghana. Sameera Abubakar who is currently the Managing Director Guarantee Impex Global Services ltd, founder and CEO Samaan Intercontinental Resource Ltd which has subdivision in Restaurant, Catering Services, Interior and Exterior deco. She is also a Director at Brainstorm IT Solution(software developer) which provide E-solutions, Director Amneef Event center. She is a certified Graduate of NIIT (Oracle 11i ), An agent of (APCON) Advertising Practitioner Council of Nigeria, kano Deputy coordinator and Council Member NACCIMA Youth Entrepreneurs, Member of (KACCIMA) Kano Chamber of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture under (NACCIMA) Nigeria Association of Chambers of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture.
